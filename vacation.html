Why I wanted to go to Disneyland

You might think that it's because I'm a massive disney fan and it's my dream to see my childhood shows in one massive theme park. It's not actually, as I didn't want many popular franchises that Disney released, the only notable ones I watched were Gravity Falls and Phineas and Ferb.

The REAL reason I wanted to go here was for a completely different reason... Band . In my school's band program, each year the band would go on a trip to Disneyland together, funded by the program. The problem was, students were also expected to pay a trip fee, which was fair enough. But the price was quite expensive, enough to where asking my mom was most likely going to result in a no.

So I never bothered to ask my mom for my years in Band, as I was convinced she would deny the question. That is until ony my last year, I again passed on the trip. My mom asked where'd all my friends go and I said to Disneyland, telling her that the trip would've been too expensive for her to pay. The catch was that she would've actually paid for the trip this time around, because it was my last year of High School. Meaning if I had bothered to just ask her that year, I could've gone on a fun trip with my friends. I'm going to comitt Lego step

So yes, I'm bitter about that irony, and I want to make up my lost trip by going to Disneyland now, even though it wouldn't be the same without my friends in High School.
